ABOUT ME

I'm Ehiabhi Kingdom, a multidisciplinary designer specializing in creating immersive digital experiences that merge aesthetic excellence with functional design. With over 7 years of experience, I've developed a unique approach that combines graphic design principles, motion dynamics, and user-centered design thinking.

My work is driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to pushing creative boundaries. I believe that great design should not only captivate visually but also enhance user experience and deliver meaningful results for clients.

MY PROCESS

My design approach is methodical yet flexible, allowing for creative exploration while ensuring project goals are met efficiently.

01

Discovery

Understanding client needs, researching the market, and defining project goals and constraints.

  • Client consultation
  • Market research
  • Competitive analysis
02

Ideation

Generating concepts, sketching ideas, and exploring different creative directions.

  • Concept development
  • Mood boarding
  • Sketching & wireframing
03

Creation

Developing the chosen concept into a fully realized design with attention to detail.

  • Visual design
  • Prototyping
  • Animation & motion
04

Refinement

Testing, gathering feedback, and iterating to perfect the final deliverable.

  • User testing
  • Client feedback
  • Final delivery
